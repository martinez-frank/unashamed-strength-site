About

Faith-first training apparel for the ones who refuse to be defined by fear, failure, or the past.

This brand exists to remind people that no matter how broken, weak, or overlooked they feel, they are still chosen by God.

After walking through battles that tested my faith- including facing life-altering hardship and standing unashamed in the face of fear. I realized my calling was bigger than myself. God didn’t bring me this far to stay silent. He shaped me to lead, inspire, and to help others rise, physically, mentally, spiritually.

Unashamed is for the ones who train not just their bodies, but their character. For those who’ve been knocked down and chose to stand back up. For those who put God first and refuse to be defined by their PAST!

Romans 11:6 reminds us that grace is not earned, it’s GIVEN. And through that grace, we grow stronger.

Romans 11:6

My mission is to help people in the gym and in life believe in who God created them to be. To walk in confidence, to live with PURPOSE, to move forward without SHAME.

This isn’t just a gym brand..

It’s a movement a testimony, a declaration of faith!
